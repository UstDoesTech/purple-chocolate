About Our Products

Purple Chocolate Company is proud to partner directly with our suppliers to ensure that our products are made with the highest quality ingredients, in an ethically sourced manner. We are committed to providing our customers with a unique and luxurious experience that is unmatched by any other chocolate brand. Our products are made with the finest ingredients and are available in a wide range of rich and elegant flavours. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the best possible experience, and we are confident that you will love our products as much as we do.

Ingredients Of Our Products

Welcome to the heart of The Purple Chocolate Company, where the magic of our exquisite chocolates begins with the finest ingredients. Each component is carefully selected to ensure the highest quality and most indulgent experience. Our cocoa beans are sourced from the lush rainforests of Ivory Coast, renowned for their rich flavors and robust aroma. We use only pure, organic milk to add a creamy texture, and our sugar is sustainably harvested from the crystalline fields of East Anglia, promising sweetness that delights. At The Purple Chocolate Company, we believe that exceptional chocolate starts with exceptional ingredients, each chosen with care and responsibility, ensuring that every bite is not just delicious but also truly special.

Cocoa

Chocolate is nothing without Cocoa. All our cocoa is sourced ethically and sustainably directly from farmers.

Sugar

The sweetness of our chocolate comes from sugar and goes into everything, even dark chocolate. All our sugar is sourced direct from farmers.

Milk

For the creamy mouth feel, we add milk to our chocolate (but not to our dark chocolate). Our milk is sourced direct from farmers in the UK and France.

Love

Love goes into everything we do, from working with our farmers to source ingredients, through to our retail partners. Love is our gift to the world.
